Adhesive Solutions

From structural bonding to flexible laminating systems, HITAC Adhesives provides tailored solutions for your specific performance and processing requirements.

Structural Adhesives

High-strength systems engineered for demanding mechanical performance, impact resistance, and long-term durability.

  • Metal, composite, and engineered substrate bonding
  • Excellent shear and peel strength
  • Optimized cure profiles for your process

Laminating & Packaging

Flexible laminating adhesives designed for converters and packaging applications requiring clarity, flexibility, and chemical resistance.

  • Solvent-based and solvent-free technologies
  • Food and industrial packaging
  • Fast line speeds and strong bonds

Construction & Building

High-performance adhesives for panel bonding, flooring, insulation, and envelope systems.

  • Weather and moisture resistant
  • Compatible with a range of substrates
  • Low VOC and specialty formulations
